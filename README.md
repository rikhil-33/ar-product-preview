# AR Product Preview 🛍️

> Try before you buy — browser-based Augmented Reality for online shopping.

---

## The Problem

Online shopping has one fundamental flaw — you can't truly see a product before buying it. Studio photos don't show real size, texture, or how something looks in your actual space. This leads to hesitation, wrong purchases, and returns.

## The Solution

AR overlays a digital product onto the real world through your camera. Most AR solutions need heavy backends or native app installs. This project delivers the same experience entirely **in the browser** — no app, no plugins, no installs.

---

## Features

- 🔄 **3D Product Viewer** — drag, rotate, and zoom products in real-time
- 📷 **AR Try-On Mode** — uses device camera to place products in your real environment
- ⚡ **Browser-native** — built with React.js and Tailwind CSS, works on any device

## Why I Built This

I wanted to solve a problem I actually experience — buying something online, it arrives, and it looks nothing like the photo. AR in the browser felt like the right answer, and I wanted to prove it could work without an app install.
